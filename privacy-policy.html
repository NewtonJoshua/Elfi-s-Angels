1. Information We Collect

At Elfi's Angels, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect the following types of information:

Analytics Information:

  • Website Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, device type, browser information
  • Interaction Data: Which puppy profiles you view, buttons clicked, contact attempts
  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized), location (city/region level), page load times
  • Device Information: Screen size, operating system, mobile vs desktop usage

Contact Information:

  • WhatsApp Communications: When you contact us via WhatsApp
  • Inquiry Details: Questions about specific puppies or breeding programs
  • Customer Preferences: Puppy color, size, or other preferences you share

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Business Operations:

  • Puppy Matching: Help you find the perfect puppy based on your preferences
  • Customer Service: Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Business Communication: Update you about available puppies and breeding programs

Website Improvement:

  • User Experience: Improve website performance and mobile experience
  • Content Optimization: Understand which puppy information is most helpful
  • Technical Enhancement: Fix bugs and optimize loading speeds

Analytics & Insights:

  • Business Intelligence: Understand customer preferences and behavior
  • Marketing Optimization: Improve our online presence and reach
  • Service Enhancement: Better serve future customers based on data insights

3. Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website:

What Google Analytics Tracks:

  • Page Views: Which pages you visit and for how long
  • Puppy Interest: Which puppy profiles receive the most attention
  • User Journey: How you navigate through our website
  • Device Usage: Mobile vs desktop preferences
  • Geographic Data: General location (city/region level only)

Privacy-First Approach:

  • IP Anonymization: Your IP address is anonymized before processing
  • No Personal Data: We don't track personally identifiable information
  • Aggregate Data Only: We analyze patterns, not individual behavior
  • Cookie Consent: Analytics cookies expire after 2 years

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and improvement (anonymized data)
  • WhatsApp/Meta: When you initiate contact through WhatsApp
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Partners: Veterinarians or pet services that may help your puppy (with your consent)

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

Opt-Out Options:

  • Analytics Opt-Out: Use browser settings to disable analytics tracking
  • Cookie Control: Manage cookies through your browser preferences
  • Communication Preferences: Unsubscribe from any communications

Data Access & Control:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure Hosting: Website hosted on secure Firebase platform
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
