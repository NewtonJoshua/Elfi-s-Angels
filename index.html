About Us

Champion bloodline meets professional care - bringing you high quality, posh companions for your family

Premium Quality & Care

At Elfi's Angels, we absolutely love raising premium Mini Poodle puppies! Our high quality puppies come from amazing AKC American Champion bloodlines with parents imported from the USA. We follow all the KCI guidelines to make sure every puppy is super healthy, friendly, and well-behaved. These posh puppies come from fantastic families, making them perfect premium companions for anyone looking for a loving, wonderful furry friend.

AKC American Champion Bloodlines

Our parent dogs are imported from the USA with AKC certification and champion backgrounds, giving you confident assurance of premium quality, friendly personalities, and beautiful looks in every puppy.

KCI Certified

All our puppies come with official KCI certification, so you know you're getting a genuine, high quality puppy with proper documentation.

Loving Home Environment

Our dogs are raised with lots of love and care in our home, not in backyards or puppy mills. We feed them amazing food from Origen and Farmina brands plus high quality meat, fruits, and very little grains for the best nutrition. All puppies learn to use a potty tray, which makes them perfect for apartment living! They're also great for houses with yards. These pups are super active, playful, and always ready for fun!

Responsible Care

We really care about raising healthy, happy puppies! We do all the health testing to make sure our dogs are in great shape, and every puppy gets checked by KCI judges and experienced vets. This way you know your puppy will look, act, and be just like a perfect Mini Poodle should be!

Health & Vaccinations

All our puppies are fully vaccinated and get complete health checkups with all the medical paperwork you need!

Shipping Available

We can ship anywhere in India. Transportation charges extra. Safe and comfortable delivery arrangements.
